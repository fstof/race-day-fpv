About Race Day FPV

Race Day FPV is a community based app where anyone can create events / race-days. Anyone can then join your event and "check-in" on the day confirming your attendance.

Note:

This app is still under heavy development and stuff will change. If you have any suggestions or improvements or bugs, please let me kow.
